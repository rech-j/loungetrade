I.

Overview

Lounge Coin ("we", "us") is a private web application for coin trading and games among members. This policy describes what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

II.

Data We Collect

  • ♦ Account information — username, email address, and password (hashed) when you register directly.
  • ♦ Google account data — if you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.
  • ♦ Profile data — display name and avatar image you choose to upload.
  • ♦ Activity data — coin transactions, game history, and leaderboard rankings generated by your use of the platform.

III.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate Lounge Coin:

  • ♦To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • ♦To record and display coin balances and transactions
  • ♦To facilitate real-time games between members
  • ♦To display your username and balance on the leaderboard

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

IV.

Google OAuth

When you use "Continue with Google," Google shares your email address, name, and profile picture with us under Google's Privacy Policy. We only request the email and profile scopes — we do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, or any other Google services.

V.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

VI.

Security

Lounge Coin is served over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Access to the server and database is restricted to authorized personnel only.

VII.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data can be directed to the site administrator via jeremyrrech@gmail.com.
